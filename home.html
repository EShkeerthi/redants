It’s all about students identifying themselves beyond Academics but towards their interest, passion and admiration. RED ANTS is a innovative thought from SASI Institute of Technology and  Engineering to bring the best out of every enthusiastic student and make him “Earn while U Learn” ideology. The mainstream idea behind this is to enhance the creative thought process of every student and drift campus culture. A New Trend we are about to bring in SASI is named after ANTS which is labelled as A NEW TEAM OF SASI. 

 

 

RED is not about colour but a three point scale transforming student into a scholar.

 

R is for research, any student who would like to go for higher education; we drive his free space towards his goals by making him work under the privileges that fulfil his goals.

 

E is for Entrepreneur when in a student want to stand by himself in any business we give him opportunity to work and see the true shades of risks and benefits before he start up. Build up confidence in the same field by exposing him to the true world.

 

D is for Development, it might be a product, idea or skill set and sometimes necessary attitude for the competitive world out there. We try to nurture the student to develop himself ready for the world.
